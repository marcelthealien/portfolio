The Book of Hester Issue 1 Graphic Novel

2016

It’s not that Hester was ever looking for Evil. In fact, Hester had no Evil intentions whatsoever. It just so happened, however, that Evil seemed to appear wherever Hester went. . . Hester and the Search for Truth is the first of three issues of The Book of Hester. Hester is an impure Hybrid, product of both Evil and Good. After eating the Fruit of Knowledge, mistaking it for an apple, Hester becomes burdened with the feeling of Shame. In order to reconcile and be rid of this feeling, he sets out to consult Truth. But where this Truth is to be found, he soon discovers, nobody has a clue. In this first issue is the tale of Hester’s adventures (and misadventures) during his search for Truth. He encounters all kinds of lovely (and not so lovely) creatures of the Sphere (and from beyond the Sphere). The Novel consists of Six parts. The full graphic novel is available in digital version on request, the hard copies were hand made at a limited edition.
