Architecture for Art

Marcel studied Architecture at the University of Cape Town and at the same time worked part-time at Bronze Age Sculpture House. The lessons and insights he gained from both architecture and sculpture have enriched and fueled his passion for visual art. After completing his undergraduate (2017) he worked for the film industry in Cape Town (2018) as a draftsman in the construction department and later as a set designer in the art department where he further gained inspiration to make architecture for art purposes. After one year abroad in South America (2019) he has returned to Bronze Age (2020) and enjoys working for the studio full-time (2021). Today, in art, he is interested in sculpture, print-making and architectural fictions. He loves and draws inspiration from the works of Iakov Chernikov and MC Escher.
